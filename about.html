Who We Are
A collection of car customisation products built for car lovers.
The Stance Customs was founded by passionate car enthusiasts right here in Gaborone, Botswana. We believe every car has untapped potential.
We source premium parts including coilovers, air suspension kits, custom mags, modified body panels, and more — all tailored to the stance culture.
Our goal is simple: give local car lovers access to the best aftermarket parts without having to go abroad.
Find Us
Based in Gaborone, we serve clients across Botswana. Reach out via phone or email to discuss your build and place an order.

Whether you're after a subtle drop or a full show-car build — we've got you covered.
